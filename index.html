    Beginner Conversation

    German Time: Official & Unofficial

    Learn to read and say time in German using official time (digital style) and unofficial spoken time (nach, vor, halb). A live clock is included.

    Quick difference

    Official: `15:20` → `fünfzehn Uhr zwanzig`
    Unofficial: `15:20` → `zwanzig nach drei`

    Interactive Clock

    Set any time and see both German styles.

    15:20
    3:20 PM

    German Time Phrases

    Compare official and everyday spoken German.

    Beginner patterns

    • nach = after/past (e.g. zehn nach drei)
    • vor = before/to (e.g. zehn vor vier)
    • halb vier = 3:30 (half to four)
    • Viertel nach/vor = quarter past / quarter to
